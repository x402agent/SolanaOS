HOW ORE v2 MINING WORKS

Deploy SOL on the 5×5 Grid

Each round lasts 60 seconds. Deploy SOL on one or more of the 25 blocks. You can concentrate on a single block or spread across multiple blocks for different risk/reward profiles.

Random Block Selection

At the end of each round, the ORE program generates a random number to select one winning block. This selection cannot be predicted or manipulated. All SOL from the 24 losing blocks is collected.

Proportional Rewards

The collected SOL redistributes to miners on the winning block proportionally based on their stake. Additionally, ~1 ORE is minted per round and distributed to winners.

Motherlode Jackpot

Each round adds 0.2 ORE to the Motherlode pool. There's a 1 in 625 chance (0.16%) it triggers, distributing the entire pool to that round's winning miners. Average trigger: ~125 ORE jackpot.

REFINING & STAKING

REFINING

Refining claims mined ORE to your wallet with a 10% fee. The fee redistributes to all miners with unclaimed balances proportionally — creating a compounding incentive to delay claiming. The more unclaimed ORE you hold, the larger your share of redistribution fees.

STAKING

Lock refined ORE to earn yield from protocol revenue. 10% of mining fees fund automated ORE buybacks. Of bought-back ORE, 90% is burned and 10% is distributed to stakers as yield.

Risk Warning: ORE mining involves financial risk. Only 1 of 25 blocks wins each round. The other 24 blocks lose their deployed SOL. Most rounds result in losses. Never deploy more than you can afford to lose.
