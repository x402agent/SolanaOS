## ♟️ Playing Chess

### **Quick Match**

The fastest way to start playing:

1. Tap **"Quick Match"** on home screen
2. Choose time control:
   - **Bullet**: 1 minute
   - **Blitz**: 3 or 5 minutes
   - **Rapid**: 10 or 15 minutes
   - **Classical**: 30+ minutes
3. Wait for opponent matching
4. Game starts automatically!

### **Create Custom Game**

For more control:

1. Tap **"Create Game"**
2. Set options:
   - **Time Control**: Choose your pace
   - **Wager Amount**: 0 to 10 SOL
   - **Public/Private**: Anyone can join or invite-only
   - **Color**: White, Black, or Random
3. Tap **"Create"**
4. Share game link or wait for players

### **Join Existing Game**

Browse available games:

1. Go to **"Games"** tab
2. Filter by:
   - Time control
   - Wager amount
   - Player rating
3. Tap a game to view details
4. Tap **"Join"** to accept

### **Making Moves**

```
1. Tap the piece you want to move
   → Legal squares highlight in green
   
2. Tap the destination square
   → Move is sent automatically
   
3. Wait for opponent's move
   → You'll get a notification

4. Repeat until checkmate!
```

### **Game Controls**

During a game, you can:

- **Offer Draw**: Both players must accept
- **Resign**: Forfeit the game
- **Request Takeback**: Undo your last move (opponent must accept)
- **Report Abuse**: Flag inappropriate behavior
- **Leave Game**: Exit without resigning (timer continues)

### **Game Results**

Games end when:

- ✅ **Checkmate**: Winner takes wager
- 🤝 **Draw**: Wager split 50/50
- ⏰ **Time Out**: Opponent wins on time
- 🏳️ **Resignation**: Opponent wins
- 🚫 **Abandonment**: Auto-loss after 5 minutes

## 💰 Wagering SOL

### **Setting Up Your Wallet**

Before wagering:

1. **Add SOL** to your wallet:
   - Buy in-app with card (MoonPay/Mercuryo)
   - Transfer from exchange
   - Receive from friends

2. **Check Balance**:
   - Tap your profile icon
   - View SOL balance
   - See pending wagers

### **Creating a Wager Game**

1. Tap **"Create Game"**
2. Toggle **"Add Wager"** ON
3. Enter amount (0.01 - 10 SOL)
4. Confirm transaction in wallet
5. SOL locks in escrow smart contract
6. Wait for opponent to match wager

### **Joining a Wager Game**

1. Browse games with wagers
2. Check wager amount
3. Tap **"Join & Match Wager"**
4. Approve transaction
5. Your SOL locks in escrow
6. Game starts!

### **Winning Payouts**

When you win:

1. **Instant**: SOL automatically sent to your wallet
2. **Fee**: 2% platform fee
3. **No Delay**: Funds available immediately
4. **Receipt**: View transaction on Solana Explorer

**Example:**
```
Wager: 1 SOL each = 2 SOL pot
Winner gets: 1.96 SOL (2% fee)
Platform fee: 0.04 SOL
```

### **Safety Features**

- 🔒 **Smart Contract Escrow**: SOL locked until game ends
- ✅ **Verified Games**: On-chain proof of every move
- 🚫 **No Cheating**: Move validation prevents illegal moves
- 💸 **Auto-Refund**: If game abandoned, wagers refunded

## 🎤 Voice Agent

### **What is the Voice Agent?**

Talk to an AI chess coach during your games! Powered by Grok Voice API.

### **How to Use**

1. **Start Voice Agent**:
   - Tap the floating 🎤 button
   - Grant microphone permission
   - Voice agent activates

2. **Ask Questions**:
   ```
   You: "What's the best move here?"
   Grok: "Knight to F3 defends well and develops..."
   
   You: "Am I winning?"

   You: "Explain the Sicilian Defense"
   Grok: "The Sicilian is White's most popular response..."
   ```

3. **Get Real-Time Commentary**:
   - Automatic commentary after each move
   - Position evaluation in plain English
   - Strategic advice

Choose your coach's personality:

**To change:**
1. Tap voice agent button

Supported commands:

## ❓ FAQ

### **General**

**Q: Is Solana Chess Club free to play?**  
A: Yes! You can play unlimited games for free. Wagering is optional.

**Q: Do I need a crypto wallet?**  
A: No, you can use demo mode or email login. A wallet is auto-created for you.

**Q: What's the minimum wager?**  
A: 0.01 SOL (about $0.50-$2 depending on SOL price).

**Q: Are games fair?**  
A: Yes! All games use validated chess rules. Moves are verified on-chain.

### **Technical**

**Q: What if I lose connection during a game?**  
A: You have 5 minutes to reconnect. Clock keeps running.

**Q: Can I play on multiple devices?**  
A: Yes! Your account syncs across all devices.

**Q: Does the app work offline?**  
A: You can play against AI offline. Multiplayer requires internet.

**Q: What's the app size?**  
A: About 150MB on iOS, 120MB on Android.

### **Wagering**

**Q: How long until I receive winnings?**  
A: Instantly! SOL is sent to your wallet when the game ends.

**Q: What happens if both players abandon?**  
A: Wagers are refunded minus network fees.

**Q: Can I cancel a wager after joining?**  
A: No, once you join, you're committed. Choose carefully!

**Q: What's the maximum wager?**  
A: Currently 10 SOL per game.

### **AI & Features**

**Q: Is the voice agent always free?**  
A: Yes, unlimited voice agent usage for all users!

**Q: Can the AI cheat for me?**  
A: No, the AI provides analysis only. You must make all moves.

**Q: How accurate is move analysis?**  
A: Very accurate! Powered by Stockfish 16 and Grok AI.

**Q: Can I turn off AI features?**  
A: Yes, in Settings → AI Features → Disable.

### **NFTs**

**Q: How do I get my first NFT pieces?**  
A: Win your first game to receive a free starter set!

**Q: Can I sell my NFTs?**  
A: Yes! List them on Magic Eden or Tensor.

**Q: Do NFTs affect gameplay?**  
A: No, they're purely visual. All players have equal gameplay.

### **Tournaments**

**Q: Can beginners join tournaments?**  
A: Yes! We have beginner-friendly tournaments.

**Q: What if I can't finish a tournament?**  
A: You'll forfeit remaining games. Entry fee is non-refundable.

**Q: How are pairings determined?**  
A: Swiss system matches you with players of similar scores.

### **Account & Safety**

**Q: How do I reset my password?**  
A: If using email: Tap "Forgot Password" on login screen.

**Q: Can I change my username?**  
A: Yes, once every 30 days. Go to Profile → Edit → Username.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**  
A: Settings → Account → Delete Account. This is permanent!

**Q: Is my wallet data secure?**  
A: Yes! We never store private keys. Wallets are non-custodial.

### **Support**

**Q: How do I contact support?**  
A: In-app: Settings → Help & Support → Contact Us  
Email: support@solanachessclub.com

**Q: Where can I report bugs?**  
A: Settings → Help & Support → Report Bug  
Or: GitHub Issues

**Q: Is there a community?**  
A: Yes! Join our Discord: discord.gg/solanachess
